Your Step-By-Step Journey

1
Land on the Canvas (The Homepage)
You arrive at the Antara home screen. You will see a beautiful spinning celestial clock representing time orbits, or a breathing grid representing spatial patterns. They represent the same core concept: many ancient calculations working together in harmony.
Select your local currency region (India · ₹ · UPI or World · $ · Card) and select either English or Hindi as your preferred reading language.
💡 Account-Free: There are no usernames, passwords, or logins required. Your privacy is absolute.
2
Select Your Personal Maps
You will see three premium flagship packages and a collapsible archive of nine modular indexes. Click any button to select that map. The card will highlight with a warm saffron border to show it is added to your track. You can add or remove modules freely.
Not sure where to begin? We recommend starting with Life Architecture (for your comprehensive natal blueprint) or Energy Cycles (the most immediately practical).
💡 Duo-Chart: If you select a compatibility report, a companion input section will safely and automatically slide open.
3
Anchor Your Birth Coordinates
Enter your full name given at birth (exactly as written on your birth certificate), your date of birth, your time of birth (select as accurately as possible; if completely unknown, leave it blank), and your place of birth (as you type, select your city from the instant lookup dropdown).
💡 Why your Birth Name? Nicknames and current married names are useful, but your birth certificate name represents your foundational vibrational blueprint.
4
Compile and Checkout
Your total updates instantly as you add or remove reports. Click the Pay button to process your order securely via Razorpay UPI (for India) or our secure stripe-level checkout (internationally). Once payment clears, your dynamic, high-resolution PDF download links are compiled and generated instantly.
💡 Instant Access: Your reports are stored locally on your device for immediate reflection, and a backup link is sent to you.
5
Read and Reflect
Each report is designed with premium accordion panels. This keeps the text beautifully structured so you don't feel overwhelmed. Read each section slowly from top to bottom, checking out the visual charts and graphs before diving into the deep text analysis.
💡 How to Read: Think of these reports as an objective mirror showing your inner habits, not as rigid predictions of your future.
