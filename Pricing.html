Antigravity's Assessment

The Strategic Discount catch:
Offering the 999/- report for 333/- directly to Digital Print Shops restricts pricing flexibility. A **999/- report for 666/-** (approx. 33% discount) is highly appealing, represents strong value, and leaves ample room for performance-based additional commission tiers (e.g., an extra 5% off once they pass ₹25K sales volume).

Dynamic Cart Integration:
We implemented quantity discount slabs directly in `index.html`. Users buying 2 reports get 15% off, 3 reports get 25% off, and 4+ reports get 33.3% off (exact ₹666 per report target), dynamically showing the discount active badge to incentivize AOV growth.

Strategic Rule: Lock in beautiful volume pricing (₹666/report) while holding future commission levers in reserve to motivate top-performing print partners.
3. Our Strategic Recommendations

1. Lead Magnet Mechanics

Make the **Biorhythm/Energy Cycle Profile (04)** completely free (or email-gated). It is highly interactive and scientifically approachable, making it the perfect landing-page hook to collect leads, who are then immediately presented with the premium packages during their personalized reading flow.

2. Curated Flagship Simplification

Having too many choices creates decision paralysis. Instead of 8 small bundles, keep **3 primary Flagship Combo Sets** in `indexCombos.html` (Identity, Relationships, and Ultimate Universe). This simple three-option choice keeps user flow clean and speeds up conversions.

3. B2B Corporate Upsell

Keep the **Corporate Strengths Analysis (06)** out of baseline consumer bundles. Position it as a high-end stand-alone purchase at ₹4,999 / $149.99, targeted directly at founders, co-creators, and business entities looking for enterprise timing matrices.
