The Astronomical Ephemeris

An Ephemeris is a table of coordinates tracking planetary positions in the sky. By dividing the 360-degree journey into twelve equal sectors of 30 degrees, we trace exact planetary positions. Fast-moving personal planets (such as the Moon, Mercury, and Mars) pass regularly over the slower, multi-year cycles of Jupiter and Saturn, creating active windows of opportunity or focus.

The Timing Overlay

The double-ring wheel compares your permanent baseline potential with dynamic, real-time planetary transits. The Inner Ring is your birth blueprint—fixed at the moment of your birth. The Outer Ring represents current planetary positions moving in real-time across the sky. When a transiting planet aligns with a placement in your birth chart, an Aspect occurs. A Conjunction (0 degrees) concentrates focus on a single theme, while an Opposition (180 degrees) creates a balance point that invites reflection, boundary setting, and equilibrium.
