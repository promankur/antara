Why this exists

Built by a rationalist.
For anyone who is curious.

The creator of Antara is known among his friends as a rationalist. The first question they asked was the obvious one.

"I didn't enter the belief system. I built the instrument. There is a difference."

— The guiding principle behind Antara

Four billion people worldwide navigate their lives using some form of astrological, numerological, or cyclical framework. That is not a fringe phenomenon — it is a civilisational fact that spans every culture, every century, and every level of education.

A rationalist's best contribution to this space is not mockery, and not conversion. It is precision — making sure the numbers, at least, are exactly right.

What Antara is

The instrument,
not the interpretation.

Every tradition has its own mathematics. Antara calculates those numbers with full technical rigour — the lagna degree, the nakshatra, the Life Path, the Kua, the Day Master, the critical days — all computed correctly, for any person, on any date.

What you do with those numbers — believe them, consult a pandit, discuss them with a friend, or simply file them away — is entirely your decision. Antara ends where interpretation begins.

  • ◇ Tradition-based, not invented. Every calculation follows the canonical method of its school — Lahiri ayanamsha for Vedic, Placidus houses for Western, classical Vimshottari for dashas, sequential assignment for Pythagorean numerology.
  • ◇ Mathematically rigorous. The same birth data run through five numerological schools and four horoscopic traditions. If the traditions are telling the truth, they should converge — now you can actually check.
  • ◇ Interpretive, not predictive. This is not a disclaimer. It is the design. Prediction requires belief. Calculation requires only mathematics. Antara only does the second.
  • ◇ Your pandit deserves accurate data. Most practitioners receive incomplete or approximate birth data. Antara gives you — and them — the precise technical foundation that any school of thought should be working from.
